Puzzle 001 · Logic · 8–15 min

Five Doors

difficulty

Five doors stand before you. Exactly one is safe.

Each door carries a plaque:

  1. 1

    The safe door has an odd number.

  2. 2

    Door 1 is not safe.

  3. 3

    Exactly one of plaques 1 and 2 is true.

  4. 4

    The safe door is beside door 5.

  5. 5

    Door 3 is safe.

✦

Exactly two plaques are true, and the safe door’s own plaque is false.

Which door do you open?

    The clean route

    Door 5 is safe.

    Test each possible safe door against both constraints. Doors 1 and 2 produce exactly two true plaques, but each makes its own plaque true. Doors 3 and 4 produce three true plaques. Only door 5 leaves exactly plaques 1 and 2 true while plaque 5 remains false.
